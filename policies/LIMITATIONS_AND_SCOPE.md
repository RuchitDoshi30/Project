# 🚧 Limitations & Scope  
## Placement Preparation Platform for Engineering Students

---

## Included Scope
- Coding practice (logic-based)
- Aptitude assessment
- Admin-controlled system
- Progress tracking

---

## Excluded Scope
- Online compiler
- Automated judging
- Leaderboards
- Payment systems

---

## Justification

These limitations reduce complexity, improve security, and keep the project aligned with academic objectives.
